About

About company page

Smart Corner is a fast growing Web Development company based in Yerevan, Armenia.
Smart Corner was founded in 2015 in Armenia and has its branch in the USA, as well.
The range of the services we offer include Website development, Web application development,
Web design, Redesign, Maintenance,Web Hosting, Graphic Design, Logo Design, SEO.
We work with both local business owners and international companies.

The most important things for us are high quality work, professional approach and desire
to excel at clients' expectations which always bring clients' satisfaction.
